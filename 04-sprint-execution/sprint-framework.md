# Sprint Framework & Delivery Cadence

## Sprint Length

* **Duration:** 2 weeks
* **Sprint Start:** Monday
* **Sprint End:** Friday (end of week 2)

---

## Agile Ceremony Schedule

### Sprint Planning

* **When:** Monday (Sprint start)
* **Duration:** 2–3 hours
* **Participants:** Scrum Master, Product Owner, All Teams
* **Purpose:**

  * Confirm sprint goal
  * Review backlog readiness
  * Commit to sprint scope
  * Validate capacity and dependencies

---

### Daily Standups

* **When:** Daily (Mon–Fri)
* **Duration:** 15 minutes
* **Focus:** Progress, blockers, dependencies

---

### Backlog Grooming / Refinement

* **When:** Every Thursday
* **Duration:** 60–90 minutes
* **Purpose:**

  * Prepare upcoming work
  * Estimate stories
  * Identify dependencies
  * Enforce Definition of Ready

---

### Sprint Review

* **When:** Last Friday of the sprint
* **Purpose:** Demonstrate completed work and gather feedback

---

### Sprint Retrospective

* **When:** Last Friday of the sprint (after Review)
* **Purpose:** Inspect process, identify improvements, commit to actions

---

## Capacity Planning Guidelines

* Planned capacity reduced to account for:

  * BAU work
  * Meetings
  * Cross-team dependencies
* BAU capped to protect sprint predictability

---

## Scrum Master Accountability

* Enforce ceremony cadence
* Protect sprint commitments
* Surface risks early
* Track and report delivery metrics
